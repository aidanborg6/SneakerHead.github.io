About me

Hey there, fellow sneaker enthusiasts! I'm Liam Burman, your guide to my world of kicks and collector extraordinaire. My journey into the realm of footwear started when i was only 8 years old and it has been a thrilling adventure since then. From the iconic streets of Liverpool, where I passionately cheer for my beloved team, to the inked canvases of my tattoos, each element of my life finds its expression in my ever-growing sneaker collection. These aren't just shoes to me; they're artifacts that tell tales of style, passion, and personal history. Join me on this exciting ride as we explore the stories behind the sneakers, where every pair becomes a chapter in the book of my eclectic lifestyle. Lace up your sneakers, and let's embark on this adventure together!





3 Personal favourite shoes



Air Jordan 1 X Off-White "chicago"

Air Jordan 1 x Off-White "Chicago" (2017): In the crown jewels of my collection, the Air Jordan 1 x Off-White "Chicago" holds a special place. Virgil Abloh's deconstructed design and the iconic Chicago colorway make this pair a showstopper. Every scuff, every crease tells a story of my journey, both in the sneaker world and in life. It's more than a sneaker; it's a symbol of breaking boundaries and embracing the raw beauty of imperfection.

Nike AirMag

Nike MAG (2011): Stepping straight out of the future, the Nike MAG is a timeless piece in my collection. Inspired by Marty McFly's iconic kicks from "Back to the Future," this pair not only exudes futuristic vibes but also carries a sense of nostalgia. The self-lacing technology adds a touch of magic, making it a conversation starter and a testament to the ever-evolving innovation within the sneaker culture.





Adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 Zebra

Adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 "Zebra" (2018): A true head-turner in my lineup, the Adidas Yeezy Boost 350 V2 "Zebra" is a rare gem that perfectly blends style and comfort. The striking black and white zebra-stripe pattern sets it apart, while the Boost technology ensures every step feels like walking on clouds. These Yeezys are not just about the hype; they represent the intersection of fashion, functionality, and Kanye West's avant-garde vision. From the streets to special occasions, this pair effortlessly elevates my sneaker game.
